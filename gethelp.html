Where to Get Help

Unsure of what to do?

Whether you just need somebody to listen to what you have to say or seek out new directions, AWARE 's Helpline volunteers can guide you to decide on the next step.

If you are experiencing tell-tale signs of abusive relationship, you may also contact PAVE

When family members are in dilemma, you can approach the nearest TRANS Family Service Centre or any Care Corner Centre
