Are you being abused?

Abuse can be emotional, physical, sexual or financial

Do you...

  1. ...feel afraid of your partner much of the time?
  2. ...avoid certain topics out of fear of angering your partner?
  3. ...feel that you can't do anything right for your partner?
  4. ...believe that you deserve to be hurt or mistreated?
  5. ...feel emotionally numb or helpless?

Does your partner...

  1. ...humilate or yell at you?
  2. ...constantly criticize you and put you down?
  3. ...ignore or put down your opinions or accomplishments?
  4. ...hurt you, or threaten to hurt or kill you?
  5. ...control where you go or what you do?
  6. ...keep you from seeing your friends or family?
  7. ...limit your access to money, the phone, or the car?

The more questions you answered "yes" to, the more likely it is that you are in an emotionally abusive relationship.

Types Of Abuse

EMOTIONAL

  • Cursing, Yelling, Insulting, Threatening
  • Making degrading comments
Using words or other emotional and psychological tactics with the intention of making victims feel inadequate or worthless and completely dependent.

PHYSICAL

  • Hitting, Slapping, Choking, Punching, Kicking
  • Using hands or objects as weapons or threats
These acts are meant to threaten or hurt the victim in order to keep him or her under their control. Physical abuse is usually done in an attempt at establishing control in the relationship.

  • More

SEXUAL

  • Using threats, intimidation or physical force to obtain sexual acts
  • Showing victims pornography against her will
Sexual abuse is usually committed through physical force or through drugging. In some cases, sexual abuse is done through psychological manipulation or coercion.

  • More

FINANCIAL

  • Rigidly controlling financial activities
  • Not contributing money to run the household
  • Stealing victim's money or valuables
  • Forcing her to work or denying her the right to work
The point of most types of abuse is to gain total control over the victim, money is often be used to achieve these goals.
